About Us

Registration Act, 1860

St. Joseph's School, Torai is ESTABLISHED and ADMINISTERED by DUMKA CATHOLIC DIOCESE. The School was blessed and inaugurated on 11th April, 2017 by Most Rev. Julius Marandi, Bishop of Dumka.

Dumka Catholic Diocese runs several schools in Jharkhand and West Bengal in which children belonging to every creed, social class, community and liguistic group are educated through the medium of English and Regional Languages.

The school is Christian Minority Instituition of Jharkhand under Article 30(1) of the Constitution of India. The School is intended to educate Christian children mainly, however, non-christian children may also be admitted. The religious beliefs of all the students are treated with respect.
