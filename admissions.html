Admission Process

Requirements for the submission of Application Form.

  1. A photostate copy of the Birth Certificate of the Applicant from Municipal Corporation/Block/Hospital, where the Applicant was born. Affidavit without proper documental proof shall not be entertained.
  2. Two (2) recent postal size colour photographs of the Applicant.
  3. One (1) recent post card size photograph of the Applicant with parents.
  4. A self-addressed envelop duly stamped.
  5. A Medical Certificate of fitness from doctor.
