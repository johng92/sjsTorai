Principal's Message

Welcome to our school! As the principal, I am proud of our dedicated staff and the supportive community that fosters a love of learning. Our goal is to provide a safe, nurturing environment where every student can thrive. We are committed to preparing our students for the challenges of tomorrow with a well-rounded education that emphasizes academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Who We Are

St. Joseph's School, Torai is ESTABLISHED and ADMINISTERED by DUMKA CATHOLIC DIOCESE. The School was blessed and inaugurated on 11th April, 2017 by Most Rev. Julius Marandi, Bishop of Dumka.
